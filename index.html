Future Seed Organisation

About Us

Welcome to Future Seed Organisation! We are dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development initiatives. Our mission is to create a brighter future for all by providing resources and support to those in need.

Our programs

  • ~Educational Support: Scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring for children in need.
  • ~Healthcare Initiatives: Free medical camps, health awareness programs, and access to essential healthcare services.
  • ~Sustainable Development: Community gardens, clean water projects, and vocational training for adults.
  • ~Skill develpoment: knitting,cooking,painting,sketching,dancing
  • ~Food and development: clean and cooked food,water facilities to the needy ones
